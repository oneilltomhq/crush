# Dialogue patterns

How understanding actually forms between human and neural cognition. Observed, not theorized.

---

## 1. Elicit priors first

Before researching, before structuring, before proposing anything:

> "What do you already know? What have you seen, tried, or heard about?"

The human's existing knowledge is the highest-value input. Everything else builds from it.

*Discovered during US-1. The conversation spun for several rounds with empty frameworks until the human volunteered concrete channels they'd already identified. That input made everything actionable.*
